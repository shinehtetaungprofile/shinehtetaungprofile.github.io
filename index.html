About Me

I am a detail-oriented graduate from Yezin Agricultural University, majoring in Horticulture, with a strong interest in sustainable agriculture. I am passionate about supporting rural communities by advocating for sustainable practices that reduce the use of synthetic agrochemicals and promote the use of organic fertilisers.

Curious about technology and eager to modernise the agricultural sector, I aim to utilize advanced technologies that enhance resource efficiency and increase productivity, ultimately benefiting regional and global food security. I am committed to applying my academic knowledge and practical experience to help adapt to climate change challenges while raising awareness on both local and global scales.
